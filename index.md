# Logic and Bounded Rationality 2026

**Online Workshop · 25–27 February 2026 · Microsoft Teams**

The workshop *Logic and Bounded Rationality 2026* brings together researchers working on logical, philosophical, and computational approaches to reasoning under resource constraints.

Topics include:
- resource-sensitive and non-classical logics
- depth-bounded and complexity-aware reasoning
- formal models of cognitive limitations
- argumentation and belief revision under constraints
- logic and AI perspectives on rational agency

Invited talks and thematic discussions. **No call for papers.**
